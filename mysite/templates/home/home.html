Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report an incident?

You can report an incident by clicking on the 'Report' button on the dashboard, filling in the details, and submitting images or videos.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we take data security seriously. All your submissions are encrypted and securely stored.

What happens if I report an issue?

We will review your report and take appropriate action, such as investigating further or notifying relevant parties.

Can I change my mind after reporting an issue?

No, you cannot change your mind after reporting an issue. If you believe there is a mistake, you should contact us immediately.

What happens if I have a question about the app?

Feel free to ask any questions or concerns you may have about the app. We are here to help.

How can I track the progress of my report?

You can track the progress of your report by visiting your dashboard and checking the status of your report.
